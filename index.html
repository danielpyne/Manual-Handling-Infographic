Manual Handling for Hair Professionals

Stay safe at work by following best practices when lifting, reaching, and moving equipment in the salon.

Legislation Overview

The UK Manual Handling Operations Regulations 1992 (MHOR) require employers to avoid hazardous manual handling where possible, assess the risk of injury, and reduce the risk by implementing safe systems of work. Employees must follow training and use provided equipment correctly.

  • Employers should provide training and equipment such as trolleys or lifting aids.
  • Employees should assess loads before lifting and request assistance if needed.
  • Risk assessments should be documented and regularly reviewed.
