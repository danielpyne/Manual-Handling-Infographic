Legislation

The Manual Handling Operations Regulations 1992 (MHOR) require employers to:

  • Avoid hazardous manual handling where possible.
  • Assess risks of injury when manual handling cannot be avoided.
  • Reduce risks by implementing safe systems of work.

Employees must follow training, use equipment properly, and report risks.
