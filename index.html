Manual Handling for Hair Professionals

Stay safe at work by following best practices when lifting, reaching, and moving equipment.

Key Tips

Lift with Legs

Bend your knees, not your back, when picking up heavy hairdressing equipment like dryers or chairs.

Keep Close

Hold tools and products close to your body to reduce strain on your back and shoulders.

Avoid Twisting

Turn your whole body instead of twisting at the waist when moving around the salon.

Use Assistance

Ask for help or use a trolley when lifting or transporting heavy items like product boxes.
